## Profil Organisasi (About Section)

- **Visi:** Menjadikan HIMATEKKOM ITS sebagai himpunan yang progresif, inklusif, dan berdampak, dengan tata kelola organisasi yang profesional serta budaya kolaborasi yang kuat dalam semangat Kabinet Sentra Sinergi.
- **Misi Strategis:**
  1. Menguatkan pelayanan internal (sistem kerja terstruktur, evaluasi, pengembangan kapasitas).
  2. Mendorong kolaborasi lintas pihak (mahasiswa, alumni, departemen, mitra).
  3. Mewujudkan transparansi informasi (publikasi, dokumentasi terpusat, akses mudah).

## Program Unggulan (Core Programs)

Categorized into three main pillars:

1. **Optimalisasi (Internal Strengthening):**
   - **CMOS (Computer Monitoring System):** Integrated monitoring and reporting for accountability.
   - **Personalia:** HR management (recruitment, upgrading, report cards, appreciation).
2. **Kolaborasi (Networking & Relations):**
   - **Hi Alumni:** Alumni relations database and experiences.
   - **Sosmas:** Social charity and external collaborations.
   - **Advocation Corner:** Welfare advocacy (UKT, FRS, scholarships).
3. **Ekspansi (Development & Impact):**
   - **COD (Career Orientation & Development):** Career prep (CV, interview, personal branding).
   - **BIOS:** Study forum on Computer Engineering professional issues.
   - **TEKKOM Insight:** Educational/creative media content.
   - **Buku Panduan Kaderisasi:** Standardization of regeneration guidelines.
   - **Website HIMATEKKOM:** The integrated digital portal and CMOS integration.

## Flagship Product: CMOS (Computer Monitoring System)

- **Description:** System for monitoring and reporting work programs in real-time.
- **Key Features:**
  - Real-time Work Program Monitoring
  - Board Member Performance Evaluation
  - Centralized Activity Documentation
  - Data-Driven Decision Making
